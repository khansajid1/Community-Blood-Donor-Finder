About BloodFinder

Connecting lives, one drop at a time.

🌟 Our Mission

At BloodFinder, our mission is simple yet vital — to make blood donation faster, smarter, and more accessible through secure and transparent technology.

💡 Why BloodFinder?

  • ⏱️ Real-time search for donors by location and blood type
  • 🔐 Secure login and user profile management
  • 📱 Mobile-first, responsive interface
  • 🔄 Easy donor registration & request tracking
  • 📊 Admin panel for managing requests and users

⚙️ How It Works

1. Register

Sign up as a donor or someone in need of blood.

2. Post / Find Requests

Post a blood request or search for nearby donors by group or location.

3. Connect & Donate

Connect securely and save lives through verified communication.

👨‍💻 Our Team

BloodFinder was created by a group of passionate developers and students who believe in using technology to solve real-life problems. From UI/UX design to backend systems, every part of this platform is crafted with care and purpose.
